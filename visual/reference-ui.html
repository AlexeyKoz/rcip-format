Ukrainian Borscht

Traditional Ukrainian red beet soup with beef, vegetables, and sour cream. A hearty, nutritious meal perfect for cold days!

30 min preparation
3 hours total
Intermediate difficulty
8 portions servings

🥬 Ingredients List

500g Beef with bone
For rich broth, use beef shank or short ribs
2 medium Beets
Fresh, firm beets for best color
300g White cabbage
Shredded into thin strips
1 large Carrots
Grated or julienned
1 medium Onion
Yellow or white onion, diced
2 medium Potatoes
Waxy variety preferred
2 tbsp Tomato paste
Adds depth and acidity
3 cloves Garlic
Fresh, minced
For serving Sour cream
Traditional garnish dairy lactose

🔧 Tools & Equipment

🍲 Large pot (5L)
🔪 Chef's knife
🥄 Wooden spoon
🧅 Grater
🍳 Frying pan
🔥 Stovetop

👨‍🍳 Cooking Instructions

1
Place beef in cold water, bring to boil and cook to make broth
1.5 hours 🌡️ 100°C
1.1 Cut meat into 2-3 cm cubes
1.2 Bring water to boil, add salt
1.3 Remove foam from surface periodically
2
Peel and grate beets, sauté with vinegar to preserve color
15 minutes 🌡️ Medium heat
2.1 Peel and grate beets on coarse grater
2.2 Sauté with 1 tbsp vinegar until softened
3
Dice onion and grate carrot, sauté until golden
10 minutes 🌡️ Medium heat
3.1 Dice onion into 5mm pieces
3.2 Add tomato paste and cook for 3 more minutes
4
Add vegetables to broth in sequence: potatoes, cabbage, then sautéed vegetables
20 minutes 🌡️ Simmer (90°C)
4.1 Add diced potatoes, cook 10 minutes
4.2 Add shredded cabbage, cook 5 minutes
4.3 Add sautéed beets and onion-carrot mix
5
Season with salt, pepper, add garlic and herbs. Let rest before serving
15 minutes rest
5.1 Add minced garlic in last 2 minutes of cooking
5.2 Turn off heat, add fresh dill and parsley
5.3 Let rest covered for 15 minutes
5.4 Serve hot with sour cream
